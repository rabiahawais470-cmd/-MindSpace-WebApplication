Trauma Recovery Reflection Guide

MindSpace — Trauma & Post-Traumatic Growth

Trauma recovery is non-linear and unique to each person. This guide invites gentle reflection on your nervous system, your anchors of safety, and the meaning you are slowly making. Use it at a pace that feels manageable. If the work becomes overwhelming, pause and reach out to a qualified trauma-informed clinician.
