Flourishing Strengths & Practices Workbook

MindSpace — Positive Psychology & Flourishing

Positive psychology is the scientific study of what makes life worth living. This workbook helps you map your character strengths, assess your wellbeing across PERMA, and design small weekly practices that nudge you from languishing to flourishing.
Your Top Character Strengths
List your top three character strengths (from the VIA survey or your own self-knowledge). Examples: curiosity, gratitude, hope, kindness, perseverance, perspective, social intelligence.

Three Good Things
List three things that went well today and why. Repeated daily for two weeks, this practice has measurably lifted mood and life satisfaction in randomised trials.
Best Possible Self
Imagine your life one year from now if everything goes as well as it realistically could. Describe what you see in two or three sentences.
Gentle Reminder
Flourishing is built from small repeated practices, not from grand transformations. Choose one practice from this workbook and commit to it for two weeks. Notice what shifts.
