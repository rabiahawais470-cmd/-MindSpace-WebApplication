💡 Sleep Hygiene Tips

  • Aim for 7–9 hours of sleep per night as an adult
  • Keep your sleep and wake times consistent — even on weekends
  • Avoid screens (blue light) for at least 60 minutes before bed
  • Keep your bedroom cool (16–18°C / 61–64°F) and dark
  • Limit caffeine after 2 pm and avoid alcohol close to bedtime
  • Try the 4-7-8 breathing technique to wind down: inhale 4s, hold 7s, exhale 8s
